How to effectevily take care of them

Taking care of a weiner dog is hard but also pretty easy if you get the hang of it. To care for a wiener dog, provide daily, low-impact exercise and a balanced diet to manage their weight and protect their back. Prevent back injuries by using ramps for furniture, avoiding jumping, and ensuring they don't overexert themselves. Grooming needs vary by coat type, but all require regular brushing, and monthly vet visits are essential for their health.
