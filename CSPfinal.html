Bonneville Salt Flats

What it is an what you can do.

The salt flats are a flat cast expanse of salt on the surface. It is formed by the evaporatin of ancient lakes, these unique landscapes are knwon for their blindigly white, hard salt crusts that create an dotherworldy and level surface. It is a popular location for speed racing, filmmaking, and photogorphy. At the Bonneville Salt Flats, you can take a drive around the landscape, or take photos. You could experience the unique landscape they have to offer. Some visitors also like to watch or attend the annual land speed racing events, and other sports. You can take photos of the vast flat terrain that brings curosity to others. The salt flats include many things, but you also have to be prepared for the conditions the salt flats brings. It could be very hot and dry in the summer, or flooded in winter and spring. there is also the risk of sudden salt of dust storms that can be very dangerous. It is crucial to check the weather forecast and road conditions before visiting.

What do you bring?

If you are wondering what to bring to the salt flats, we got your back. Here is a good list of what to bring to be prepared for anything and have the best experience you can have.

  • Plenty of water (crucial for hydration)
  • Sunscreen for the harsh sun
  • A wide brimmed hat. Essential for protection
  • sunglasses! Make sure to choose a pair that provides good UV protection
  • plenty of snacks or a full picnic lunch. There are no facilities on the flats.
