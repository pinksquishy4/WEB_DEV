Weiner Dogs

The types of weiner dogs

There are many different types of weiner dogs. Some are smooth haired, some are miniature, and some are mixed like the chiweenie.

  • Chiweenie
  • Smooth haired dachshund
  • Miniature dachshund

How to take care of them

Give them gentle exercise, healthy food, and protect their backs. Brush their fur and visit the vet often. You need to check their health a lot you do not want them to get sick. You also need to give them lots of love and attention. Weiner dogs are very social animals and need companionshop
